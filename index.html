Stay safe from HEATWAVE


- Stay indoors during peak heat hours, usually between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m.
- Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
- Wear lightweight, light-colored clothing and a wide-brimmed hat.
- Avoid strenuous outdoor activities, especially during the hottest part of the day.
- Use fans or air conditioning to cool down indoor spaces.
- Check on vulnerable individuals, such as the elderly or those with health conditions.
- Never leave children or pets in hot cars.
